Employee Benefits

We take care of our people with a comprehensive package designed to support your health, wealth, and happiness.

What We Offer

🏥

Medical Aid

Full medical cover for you and your dependents including dental and optical.

💰

Pension Fund

Company-matched retirement contributions of up to 10% of your salary.

🏖️

Annual Leave

21 days paid leave per year, with additional family responsibility and sick leave.

🏠

Remote Work

Flexible hybrid work arrangements and a home-office equipment allowance.

🎓

Study Assistance

Up to M8,000 per year for approved courses and professional certifications.

🍽️

Meal Allowance

Daily meal vouchers and subsidised canteen access at our main offices.

🧘

Wellness Programme

Free access to EAP counselling, gym memberships, and mindfulness apps.

👶

Parental Leave

4 months fully paid maternity leave and 4 weeks paternity leave.

🚗

Travel Allowance

Monthly transport subsidy and company car for senior roles.
