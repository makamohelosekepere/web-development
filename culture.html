Our Company Culture

We believe great work begins with great people — supported by a culture of trust, inclusion, and purpose.

Who We Are

Built on Shared Values

At MKSCorp, culture isn't a poster on the wall — it's lived every day through how we collaborate, celebrate wins, and support each other through challenges. Our diverse team spans 12 countries, bringing together unique perspectives to solve complex problems.

Our Core Values

🤝 Integrity

We do what we say. Transparency and honesty are non-negotiable at every level of the organization.

🌍 Inclusion

Every voice matters. We champion diversity and create environments where all employees feel they belong.

💡 Innovation

We encourage bold thinking, embrace calculated risk, and reward curiosity and creative problem-solving.

🌱 Growth

We invest in our people's professional and personal development — because your growth is our growth.

⚖️ Balance

Sustainable performance matters. We promote healthy work-life integration and wellbeing for all staff.

🏆 Excellence

We hold ourselves to high standards and celebrate achievements — both big milestones and small wins.
